### Overview
The complex was found to have an electronic conductivity of 4 × 10−7 S cm−1 and total conductivity of 2 × 10−6 S cm−1 at room temperature, which allows the construction of a 2.7 μm thick dense cathode.  The flexible P4VP·ICl|LIPON|Li battery was prepared by simply replacing the rigid substrate with a flexible polyimide substrate and the as-prepared battery can be bent 180° while maintaining electrochemical performance.

### Key contributions
- Identified an amorphous solid cathode material that allows non-PVD deposition to increase the throughput of cathode preparation and allow wider processing possibility
- Reduced the thickness of LIPON electrolyte by introducing an isotropic, amorphous cathode, which further lowers the manufacturing time and cost.
- First to apply iCVD to fabricate a cathode candidate for Li batteries.
