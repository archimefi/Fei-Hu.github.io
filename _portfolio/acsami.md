### Overview
An Artificial solid electrolyte interface (ASEI) layer consisting of a lithium phosphorus oxynitride (LiPON) thin film electrolyte and gold-alloying interlayer was developed and shown to promote the electrodeposition of smooth, homogeneous, mirror-like Li metal morphologies. 

### Key contributions
- The Au layer alloyed with Li, reducing the nucleation overpotential and resulting in a more spatially uniform metal deposit.
- The LiPON layer provided a physical barrier between the Li metal and aprotic liquid electrolyte..
- The layers were prepared by DC/RF physical vapor deposition (PVD) techniques, providing a new strategy in the design of artificial SEIs to enable Li metal anodes with practical areal capacities by applying PVD.
