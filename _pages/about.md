- 📍 Postdoctoral Fellow @ Johns Hopkins University  
- ⚡ Focus: Functional coatings by PVD and CVD, Li battery, operando diagnostics, aotumation robots
- 💬 <div class="center-text">Materials scientist with 7+ years’ experience bridging semiconductor thin films and next-gen batteries. I specialize in interfacial engineering—ultrathin PVD/CVD coatings and multifunctional layers (PVD (sputtering,E-beam and thermal evaporation) and CVD) that tune charge/ion transport, suppress parasitic reactions, and improve optical/mechanical/thermal reliability. I connect process → structure → interphase chemistry → performance using electrochemistry (plating/stripping, EIS), operando/thermal measurements, XPS, SEM, Raman, and advanced materials characterization (synchrotron XRD, nano-CT). I bring semiconductor-style rigor to electrochemical development with DOE/SPC, Design for Six Sigma, and Python-driven high-throughput automation (Opentrons/OP2) to accelerate sample screening and make data-driven decisions.
  </div>
